    userGuide: |
      Quiet Minds has no sounds at all — no music, no beeps, no voices.

      We made this choice for a few reasons. Children use tablets in all sorts of
      places: in the back seat, in a waiting room, in a sibling's bedroom.
      An app that makes noise without warning isn't welcome in those places.

      We also wanted the app to work for every child, including children with
      sensory sensitivities who find unexpected sounds distressing, and children
      who use hearing aids or live in households where noise is a challenge.

      The silence is intentional. It means you can hand a tablet to your child
      at any moment, in any place, without reaching for the volume button first.
    knownLimitations:
      - Children who learn better with audio prompts will not get that support here
      - Text labels are provided on all images and color swatches as a substitute
